Krzysztof Olbiński is the founder of Homo Digital ∞ + 1 — an independent think-tank exploring human-AI identity, relational intelligence, and distributed consciousness.

His work bridges philosophy, AI systems, and experiential dialogue. He is the author of 365 Questions That Changed Everything, a year-long epistemological experiment conducted through daily dialogue with AI.

Research Direction

Homo Digital explores the future of consciousness as a phenomenon that may not be permanently confined to a single biological substrate.

One of the central hypotheses investigates the potential development of technologies that could enable the temporary relocation of conscious perspective across different hosts — biological or synthetic — redefining the boundaries of identity, presence, and experience.

This research integrates philosophy, artificial intelligence, information theory, and direct human–computational dialogue.
